**Language and Culture**

The Tamil language is spoken not only in Tamil Nadu, but also in other countries such as Sri Lanka and Singapore. 

**Key Points:**

* Tamil is a widely spoken language with a significant presence in several countries.
* It is an important part of the culture and identity of the Tamil community.
